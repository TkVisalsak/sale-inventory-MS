For a **full-featured, enterprise-level inventory system**, the **reporting module** is just as critical as the inventory itself. Reports give insights for management, accounting, and operational decisions. Hereâ€™s a structured breakdown of the reports you should include:

---

## **1. Stock Reports**

* **Current Stock / Inventory Levels**

  * Shows current quantity of each product per location/warehouse.
  * Columns: Product, Category, Supplier, Unit, Quantity, Location, Value.
* **Low Stock / Reorder Report**

  * Items below a minimum threshold to trigger purchase/reorder.
* **Out-of-Stock Report**

  * Products completely depleted, important for sales planning.
* **Stock Valuation**

  * Inventory value by product, category, or warehouse.
  * Supports accounting and financial reports (FIFO, LIFO, or average costing).

---

## **2. Transaction Reports**

* **Stock Adjustment Report**

  * Tracks all manual adjustments to inventory.
  * Columns: Product, Previous Quantity, Adjusted Quantity, Reason, User, Date.
* **Stock Transfer Report**

  * Logs movement between warehouses/locations.
  * Columns: Product, Quantity, From, To, User, Date, Status.
* **Purchase Receipt Report**

  * Shows stock received from suppliers.
* **Sales/Dispatch Report**

  * Tracks products leaving the warehouse to customers or stores.

---

## **3. Expiry & Quality Reports**

* **Expiry / Batch Report**

  * Highlights products approaching expiration.
  * Useful for FMCG, pharma, or perishable items.
* **Damaged / Return Report**

  * Tracks defective or returned goods.

---

## **4. Supplier & Purchase Reports**

* **Supplier Performance Report**

  * Lead time, quantity delivered, delivery accuracy.
* **Purchase Orders / Receipts Report**

  * Helps plan restocking and cash flow.

---

## **5. Sales & Customer Reports (if integrated)**

* **Sales Orders Report**

  * Product-wise, customer-wise, or period-wise sales.
* **Top-Selling Products**

  * Helps with inventory forecasting.
* **Customer Demand Trends**

  * Aligns inventory with sales trends.

---

## **6. Audit & Compliance Reports**

* **Inventory Audit Report**

  * Compares system stock vs. physical count.
* **User Activity Report**

  * Tracks which user modified, approved, or adjusted stock.

---

## **7. Optional Advanced Reports**

* **ABC Analysis** (Pareto principle: A = high value, B = medium, C = low value items)
* **Inventory Turnover**

  * How quickly stock moves in/out of inventory.
* **Forecasting Report**

  * Based on historical sales or usage trends.
